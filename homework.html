What is Ojja

Ojja is a tunisian food and it is so easy to prepare that's why i love it.

Elements needed and Steps to follow

  • Egs
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Potatos
  • Tomatos
  • Meal
  1. prepare the potatos in hot-water 😀

  2. Prepare Meal in some oil 😜

  3. Mix the Eggs with tomatos,meal,salt and potatos 😇

  4. Cook the mixed elements 😁

  5. Finally,it is ready to it 😀
